The Purpose Behind One Africa

In forming this organisation, we believe that Africa – as it is today – can only overcome its obstacles by uniting its minds. One Africa Project focuses on leadership empowerment, as well as the enhancement of cultural knowledge. Consequently, these were the principles that the co-founders, Ludo Bokuli and Rodrigue Kabuya, established the organisation upon. Together with dynamic and youthful individuals from all walks of life, the One Africa Project team was born.
