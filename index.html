Welcome To One Africa

One Africa is a multi-cultural organisation that is based and founded on principles of Ubuntu (the African philosophy of togetherness and community unity) and also secondary to this is the enhancing of leadership and managerial characteristics of young African minds.

Our Mission

Driven by the love of this beautiful continent and the passion to change its narrative, One Africa sets out to be a unifying force that is driven by youthful vigour and passion. Also with understanding that through changing the African narrative we will begin to reshape the African perspective.

Our Vision

As One Africa Project, we have envisioned a future that extends to all corners of the continent and beyond. We envision, that through the introduction and continuation of the engagement projects that will be running, we would then be able to induce a new sense of African pride, togetherness, peace, unity, change and prosperity.
